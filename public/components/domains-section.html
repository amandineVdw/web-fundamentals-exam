How the web finds its way to your favorite sites

Domain Lifecycle

Available

Domain is free to register by anyone.

Active

Registered and in use with DNS configured.

Expired

Grace period before becoming available again.

Redemption

Final chance to renew at higher cost.

DNS Explained

The Domain Name System (DNS) is like the phonebook of the internet, translating human-readable domain names to machine-readable IP addresses.
